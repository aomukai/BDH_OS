[user]what is a shape?
[Ninereeds]A shape is the form or outline of something. Balls, boxes, and signs all have shapes. Shapes can be drawn on paper or seen in the world. Shape helps people notice and describe how something looks. A shape is not a color.

[user]what is a circle?
[Ninereeds]A circle is a round flat shape with no corners. Coins, wheels, and clocks can be circles. A circle is smooth all around the edge. A circle looks round from every side. A circle is not a square.

[user]what does it mean for something to be "round"?
[Ninereeds]Round means shaped like a circle or curved all around. A ball, a coin, and the full moon can look round. Round things do not have sharp corners. Round is a word people use for both flat shapes and solid things. Round is not the same as square.

[user]what is a square?
[Ninereeds]A square is a flat shape with four equal sides and four corners. Tiles, windows, and some boxes are square-shaped. All four sides of a square are the same length. A square has straight edges, not a round edge. A square is not a circle.

[user]what is a triangle?
[Ninereeds]A triangle is a flat shape with three sides and three corners. Triangles can be big or small, but they always have three sides. Rooftops and slices of pizza can look like triangles. A triangle has fewer sides than a square. A triangle is not a square.

[user]what is a rectangle?
[Ninereeds]A rectangle is a flat shape with four sides and four corners. Doors, books, and screens are often rectangles. The opposite sides of a rectangle match in length. A rectangle is like a square, but not all sides have to be the same. A rectangle is not a triangle.
