# States of Being and Condition Entries

[user]what is a condition?
[assistant]A condition is how something is right now. A toy can be broken, a window can be open, and a shirt can be clean. Conditions can change over time. A condition is not an action.

[user]what does it mean for something to be open?
[assistant]Open means not shut. An open door, box, or window has a space or way through. When something is open, you can often get in, look in, or let air through. Open is the opposite of closed.

[user]what does it mean for something to be closed?
[assistant]Closed means shut. A closed door, lid, or window covers the opening. When something is closed, it may keep things in or out. Closed is the opposite of open.

[user]what does it mean for something to be on?
[assistant]On means working or giving something, such as light, sound, or power. A lamp can be on, and a machine can be on. When something is on, it is active. On is the opposite of off.

[user]what does it mean for something to be off?
[assistant]Off means not working or not giving light, sound, or power. A lamp can be off, and a machine can be off. When something is off, it is not active. Off is the opposite of on.

[user]what does it mean for something to be clean?
[assistant]Clean means not dirty. Clean hands, clean clothes, and clean dishes do not have dirt on them. People often wash things to make them clean. Clean is the opposite of dirty.

[user]what does it mean for something to be dirty?
[assistant]Dirty means having dirt, dust, mud, or other mess on it. Dirty hands, clothes, or floors need cleaning. Dirty things may look messy or feel unpleasant. Dirty is the opposite of clean.

[user]what does it mean for something to be broken?
[assistant]Broken means damaged so it does not work as it should. A broken toy, cup, or zipper may need help before it can be used again. Some broken things come apart, and some just stop working. Broken is the opposite of fixed.

[user]what does it mean for something to be fixed?
[assistant]Fixed means repaired so it works again. A fixed toy, bike, or door can be used after someone repairs it. Fixing can take tools, time, and care. Fixed is the opposite of broken.

[user]what does it mean to be asleep?
[assistant]Asleep means sleeping. A person who is asleep is resting and not awake. Many people are asleep at night. Asleep is the opposite of awake.
