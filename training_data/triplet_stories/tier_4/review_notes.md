## Specific things to check in Tier 4

1. **Multi-step Causal Reasoning**
- Look for multiple linked causal statements.
- Ensure the reasoning is explicit but grounded in the story action.

2. **Temporal and Logical Sequences**
- Check for use of sequence markers like "first," "after," "then," and "finally."
- Verify that multi-step processes are clear and logically ordered.

3. **Paragraph Structure and Flow**
- Confirm the use of 2–3 paragraphs for topical or logical grouping.
- Check that the flow between sentences and paragraphs feels natural and coherent.

4. **Reading Level (Grade 4–6)**
- Ensure the complexity of sentences is appropriate (not too simple like Tier 1, but not adult-level abstract).
- Look for a variety of sentence structures.

5. **Character Consistency**
- Confirm that named characters are reused correctly from Tier 2 and Tier 3.
- Check `training_data/triplet_stories/character_registry.md` if new names are introduced.

6. **Narrative vs. Explanatory Tone**
- Ensure the file doesn't drift into "encyclopedia mode."
- The goal is to teach through a scene, not through a list of facts.

7. **Vocabulary and Grounding**
- Strictly enforce Phase 1–5 and Wiki Level 1 vocabulary.
- Ensure all mentioned concepts contribute to the story's logic.
