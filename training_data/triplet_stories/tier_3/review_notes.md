## Specific things to check in Tier 3

1. **Paragraph structure**
- Ensure 2–3 small paragraphs are used to group related actions or thoughts.
- Check that the transition between paragraphs is logical.

2. **Causal reasoning**
- Verify that "because," "so," or "if... then" are used correctly to show reasoning.
- The causal chain should be grounded in the concrete scene.

3. **Contrast and Comparison**
- Check for the use of "but" or "instead."
- Ensure there is at most one "but" per story to keep the logic simple.

4. **Character reuse**
- Confirm that named characters are consistent with their Tier 2 counterparts.
- Check `training_data/triplet_stories/character_registry.md` for existing names.

5. **Elliptical dialogue**
- If dialogue is used, check if it correctly employs ellipsis while remaining understandable from context.

6. **Sentence variety**
- Look for varied sentence openings (not always starting with "The [subject]...").

7. **Vocabulary grounding**
- Strictly enforce Phase 1–5 and Wiki Level 1 vocabulary.
